Who is Christina?

I was given a simple childrens camera when I was around seven years old and I would take pictures of anything and everything. The camera I had took film and I would get so excited when my mother would take me to get it developed. Even at a young age I would arrange the photographs into themes, building my own photo albums and gifting them to my family.

I specialise in urban photography and wedding photography. I think buildings can really tell a story. I grew up in New York City and have a great love of travelling to different cities to photograph interesting buildings and locations.

After studying at the New York Institute of Photography I started to travel to different cities, building my portfolio. One of my friends that I was visiting was getting married and asked me to be a photographer for her special day and just fell in love with the whole process.
