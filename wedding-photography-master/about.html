Behind the lens

I’m Tom, and I'm so happy that you are here.

I am a wedding photographer who uses a documentary style of photography to capture your special day. I am passionate about capturing candid moments where you can feel the emotions from the image.

Your wedding is something beautiful. The feeling of happiness, and romance in the air, on such a special day and you should enjoy every minute of it.

My photos will capture the magical emotions, and allow you to relive the wonderful experience every time you look at them.

My Philosophy

Documentary wedding photography focuses on telling the whole story of your wedding.

My hands-off approach allows me to observe, and let the moments evolve naturally.

There is beauty in the natural, candid and unposed photos that cannot be replicated by the traditional methods of formal wedding photography.

It’s the little details, stories, and the quirky moments, which makes your wedding unique.

These are the memories, and photos you’ll love forever.
